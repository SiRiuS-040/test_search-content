Order-section

  • Can I return all or part of my order?

    To properly manage inventory for all online customers, an order quantity limit of 1/2 pallet per item, and re-order frequency of 15 days has been set for online orders. If customers are able to order items in full pallet quantities, please contact us regarding moving to direct service.
